void Controleur::parse ( const QString &  com)

Method that parses a QString to isolate the expressions to form Litteral objects or execute operators. If it's a Programme or an Expression, it creates the associated Litteral and pushes it in the Pile. Otherwise, it means it's one or a series of different Litterals. It splits the com parameter and analyzes each one independently.
